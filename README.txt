Floppy Flippers

This is a pinball style game where you try to hit the ball into a bell at the top of the scren.
Meanwhile, a wall is moving around the screen trying to stop you. Get at least 3 points in 1 minute
to win!


Ti play, use FloppyFlippers.exe, or open Pinball.unity in Unity


Controls:

Left - Left Flipper
Right - Right Flipper


Challenges:
Creating working (if floppy) Pinball Flippers using HingeJoints
Walls have appropriate bounciness
Game involves bouncing ball into bell at least 3 times
